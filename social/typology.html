what these types mean

this page uses shorthand labels from a few different personality frameworks. the short version is that i deeply value peace, creativity, and close, authentic connections. if you are new to typology, here is a plain-English guide to what all these letters actually say about me:

MBTI & classic jungian (ISFP / IF(S)): means i navigate the world through deep personal values and a quiet, creative awareness of my surroundings. i'm realistic and observant, but i love to follow my personal imagination.

enneagram, tritype, & subtype (9w1 962 sx/sp): my core drive is seeking inner peace and keeping things calm. the "1 wing" adds a gentle idealistic streak. "sx/sp" means i conserve my energy for a few deeply meaningful, intense one-on-one friendships rather than spreading myself thin in big crowds. the 962 tritype means i'm naturally supportive, loyal, and helpful.

socionics (SEI-0) & temperament: describes a cozy, comfort-oriented, and diplomatic personality style. i naturally focus on creating a harmonious, relaxed environment and preserving my energy battery.

attitudinal psyche & psychosophy (ELFV / 1E 2L 3F 4V): points to how i process information. Emotion is my highest priority (i'm deeply in touch with feelings), followed by a completely open, collaborative approach to Logic and discussion. physical Comfort and Willpower take a quieter, more relaxed back seat.

big 5 & MOTIVES: a personality profile that shows i am naturally reserved, calm under pressure, and highly empathetic, with a primary life drive focused on finding peace and emotional harmony.

amatorics & Interpersonal Style (PSAE / FIRO / Rhetoristics): in friendships and relationships, i am passionate and deeply loyal, but i need a lot of personal space to recharge. i communicate with a lot of heart (Pathos) and cooperate easily to keep the peace.

temporistics (PNBV) & Alignment (Neutral Good): i am naturally focused on the Present moment and what's happening right now, and i try my best to be kind, fair, and helpful to others without getting caught up in rigid rules or unnecessary drama.

about the frameworks

if you want to look up the specific theories used on this page, here is a quick summary of what each one covers:

temporistics: a model exploring how individuals prioritize and relate to time aspects (the Past, preseNt, future (B), and eternity (V)). my profile emphasizes focusing heavily on the immediate experience of the past.

attitudinal psyche / psychosophy: Systems that measure a person's level of confidence, openness, and defensiveness across four main areas: Emotion, Logic, Physics, and Volition.

MOTIVES: a framework tracking seven fundamental behavioral and lifestyle drives, highlighting a core preference for harmony and stability.

amatorics: a psychological taxonomy derived from attitudinal models that categorizes platonic and romantic relationship bonding styles.
